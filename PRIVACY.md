# Privacy Policy — Strummer

_Last updated: 2026-07-02_

Strummer is a virtual guitar. It is designed to respect your privacy.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Strummer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- No account or sign-in.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No network requests — the app works fully offline.

## Data stored on your device

The following stays entirely on your device and is never uploaded:

- **Saved chord palettes** — stored in a local database on your device.
- **Recordings** — WAV files you record are saved in the app's private storage.
  You choose if and when to share them via the system share sheet.
- **Preferences** — e.g. whether you've seen the intro screen.

Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

## Permissions

Strummer requests no runtime permissions. It plays audio through the standard
system output and records only the sound it generates internally (not your
microphone).

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the app's store
listing.
